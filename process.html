INCREDIBLE RESULTS START WITH A GOOD PROCESS

  1. Discovery

    To effectively integrate your functional requirements, we begin with an initial consultation to discuss your company’s current framework, and its vision. Following this, our team will guide you with a digital approach that is best tailored to your business model.

    Client Brief Meeting

    After our initial consultation, our team will put together a budget proposal and a project timeline that incorporates a scope of work. Your unique project timeline will be in the form of an online open document allowing both parties to review, revisise and or make adjustments where necessary.

    Proposal

    Our team will present you with a service agreement which comprises the scope of work discussed in the prior meeting, and its associated fee. Once approved, we will proceed with developing an effective strategy for your project

  2. Strategy

    Ultimately, organisation lies at the core of every successful project. Incorporating the unique facets of your business, we will provide you with a bespoke pre-build strategy.

    Communications Plan

    Our progress is your priority, that’s why we’ve created a unique project tracking system so that our clients can overlook their project each step of the way.

    Pre-Build Meeting

    In this meeting, we further review and explore the details of your business and help you establish a project vision. We will discuss your brand image, user flow, concerns and preferences. This will ensure a streamlined, successful build.

    Information Architecture

    This optional stage structures your new sitemap and the customer journey map for your website. Our team will assist you in identifying your major visitor types, and the most intuitive way to direct your visitors through key content. To optimise your Information Architecture, if applicable, we will analyse and integrate current site data and metrics. We will then review your current URL and SEO structure to generate development practices that allow for their retention.

    Content and Creative Planning

    Our project strategist will assist you with the creative processes required for developing an effective story and a satisfying experience for all visitors. A content plan and a creative plan will subsequently be written, outlining various approaches to achieve this effect. If required, we will re-write or co-write content to enhance the quality of your brand story.

  3. Design

    In the background, our team will work on a unique design to accommodate the detailed brief that was established in previous consultations. All pages and assets will be designed for your review and approval, allowing for the commencement of the construction of your website.

    A strong brand image is a key factor in business success. In this collaborative process, our team will assist in refining your brand identity for its translation into a unique website design that converges functionality and creativity.

    Wireframe UX/UI Design

    A wireframe which visualises the structure of your webpages as well as interface elements will be presented to you. The wireframe incorporates the features of your site, and their function, location, and usability. In turn, this will provide clarity on the visual representation of the user experience and interface.

    Approval

    Before we proceed to the build phase of the website, a preliminary-build consultation will be organised so that all page blueprints can be reviewed and approved by you. After the review and approval of all design assets, our team will commence with the construction of your website.

  4. Build

    Realising your vision, our team will begin to develop and build your new website as agreed upon in the design phase and scope of work.

    Custom Development and SEO

    Whilst developing your website, we will provide you with a preview link to monitor its progress, and to allow you to directly interact with the site and validate cross-browser and cross-device compatibility. Alongside the front-end development process, our team will begin the development of custom functions, bridging your company’s vision with UI and UX. Our team will also establish and integrate various on page SEO meta tags to increase the site’s usability and discovery on search engine sites.

    Content and CMS Population

    To prevent manually migrating content heavy items via CMS, we will import the items directly into a CSV file. In the case that your content is inaccessible via CSV, our team will write custom scripts to securely and accurately transfer content data to your new CMS, or manually enter the data on a case to case basis.

  5. Handover and Launch

    After populating all content assets into your website, our team will complete a quality assurance check and run a speed diagnostic to detect any technical issues. Following your approval, the latest version of the website will be finalised. This will be copied to create 2 back-up files, and our team will provide you with a unique access code to your website. Finally, the website will be made public and if required, the web domains redirected to reflect the new address
